[B]Clean Flash[/B]
1) Backup Everything (Most Important) + Latest Vendors And Firmware
2) Download ROM, Gapps, Magisk, etc.
3) Reboot to Recovery (TWRP)
4) Wipe System, Data, Cache, Dalvik Cache.
5) Flash - Vendor + Firmware + ROM + Gapps + DFE + Msgisk.
6) Once done, wipe cache.
7) Reboot to System.

[B]Dirty Flash[/B]
1) Backup Everything (Most Important) + Latest Vendors And Firmware
2) Download ROM
3) Reboot to Recovery (TWRP)
4) Wipe Cache & Dalvik Cache.
5) Flash - Vendor + Firmware + ROM + Gapps + DFE + Msgisk.
6) Once done, wipe cache.
7) Reboot to System.
